Biography

Oğuzhan Çiftçi is currently a Ph.D. candidate and a Research Assistant in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Marmara University, Istanbul. He earned his M.Sc. degree from Boğaziçi University, Istanbul, specializing in resilient distributed algorithms. He is currently conducting research as a Visiting Researcher at Brunel University London under the international fellowship program funded by CoHE Türkiye.

His current research efforts are centered on multi-agent system-based coordinated power and energy management in microgrids, with a specific focus on robust control and distributed optimization. He has a strong background in academic research and has been part of multiple TÜBİTAK-funded projects in the field of modern control theory and power systems.
