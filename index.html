Biography

Oğuzhan Çiftçi is a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Marmara University. He is currently a Visiting Researcher at the Brunel Interdisciplinary Power Systems (BIPS) Research Centre, Brunel University of London, funded by YOK GEP.

He received his MSc from Boğaziçi University in 2023, where his research was funded by TUBITAK 117E204. He completed his BSc at Marmara University in 2018 as an honor student.

His research interests focus on multi-agent systems, energy & power systems management, fault detection algorithms, modern control theory, and system modeling.
