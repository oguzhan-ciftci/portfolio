Biography

Oğuzhan Çiftçi is a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Marmara University. He received his MSc from Boğaziçi University in 2023 and his BSc from Marmara University in 2018. He is currently a Visiting Researcher at the Brunel Interdisciplinary Power Systems (BIPS) Research Centre, Brunel University of London.

His research interests include multi-agent systems, energy & power systems management, fault detection algorithms, modern control theory, and system modeling.
